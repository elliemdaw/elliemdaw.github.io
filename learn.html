I Think it's Important

Around this time of year, everyone starts to think about change.

Personally, I don't like to do New Year's Resolutions. I haven't in a few years. Instead, if something comes up that needs changing, the change should happen as soon as is reasonable. However, the end of the year is still a good time for reflection since a lot of us think in terms of yearly recaps, items accomplished, and task lists.

That begs the question... what came out of this reflection?

Well, I love to learn things. However, if that learning has no direction then it may defeat the purpose. But which direction do I want to run toward? That is a tough question to answer for someone who has so many interests. What is important for me is that instead of being overwhelmed by the possibilities or being reckless with choice, I want to be sure that every single day I learn something new. Maybe not in any specific category just yet, just something that interests me. Every day should contribute to figuring out what direction to pursue next, or will accidentally be a step in the right direction since I'll be gathering information about something I enjoy.

As I aim to learn something new every day, I'll try to keep a quick journal of some of the highlights here.

It'll be a catalog of my musings, but hopefully it will not only keep me accountable for this new action item but may also help me keep track of things I have explored and watch as my interests and direction evolve.
